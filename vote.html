VOTE FOR LEGENDARY HARDCORE

Our voting system is free from in-game rewards and benefits to reflect our commitment to vanilla Minecraft. However, if you vote regularly, you will receive perks such as:

  • Use [inv] to show off your inventory!
  • Use [ender] to show off your ender chest!
  • Use [item] to show off the item you are holding!
  • A badge next to your name showing you voted!

To maintain a vote streak you need to vote on all of the sites listed here once per day.
