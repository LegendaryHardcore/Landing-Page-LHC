ABOUT THE SERVER

Welcome to the Events Server, your ultimate getaway from the intensity of our main and respawn servers! This is the perfect spot to enjoy a dynamic lineup of minigames and staff-hosted events. Our events evolve over time, featuring everything from classic challenges to original community ideas.

Getting to the Events Server is simple! From either the main server or the respawn server, just type /server events in the chat. Your location and inventory are automatically saved exactly as they were when you left the other server, ensuring a smooth transition back whenever you're ready. Deaths here are consequence free, they won't affect your hardcore lives or experience on other servers. Switching between servers won't activate any pearl stasis setups you have in place, keeping your strategies intact.

Legendary HardCore is not an official Minecraft service. The server is not approved by or associated with Mojang or Microsoft.
