SERVER RULES

As we are a vanilla server, we have rules in place to ensure everyone is playing the game as intended

No lag machines or deliberate overuse of server resources

No use of hacked clients, external programs, X-Ray texturepacks or radar

No use of exploits or duping (except rail/carpet/ gravity/TNT)

For more information, see our rules page.

If evidence is found or presented of a player breaking these rules, bans can be issued. To make an appeal, make a ticket on the discord.

CONTACT US

The best way to get support for server issues is to join our Discord and create a support ticket after reading the #support-readme channel.
