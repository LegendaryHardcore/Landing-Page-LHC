ABOUT THE SERVER

Legendary Hardcore Respawn is a your alternative to our flagship Legendary Hardcore server—the oldest permanent death Minecraft server in existence!

While the main server provides a true permadeath experience, this respawn variant keeps everything else identical: a vast world, challenging gameplay, community events, and survival mechanics.

The key difference? Instead of a permanent ban upon death, you'll face a 30-day temporary ban.

This gives you time to reflect, plan your comeback, and jump back in once the cooldown ends. It's perfect if:

You've died on the main permadeath server and want to keep playing in a familiar environment without starting from scratch elsewhere.

You're new to hardcore survival and not quite ready to risk it all on a single life—use this as a training ground to build skills and confidence.

Legendary HardCore is not an official Minecraft service. The server is not approved by or associated with Mojang or Microsoft.
