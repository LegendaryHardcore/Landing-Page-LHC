Legendary Hardcore is the oldest permanent deathban Minecraft server. Having surpassed 16,700 unique players and 8,700 deaths, your odds of dying are 52.10%.

Our priority is to stay as close to vanilla Minecraft as possible while providing an experience that will not leave you vulnerable to hackers.

Outside of core gameplay rules, we leave everything up to our community for truly player-emergent gameplay. Players can traverse the dimensions to build civilizations together, construct various mob farms, trade amongst each other, or put it all on the line for a fight to the death.

On this server, every choice you make will be weighed, and you'd better hope you are not found wanting. There are no revives or pay-to-win options to bring you back.

We run a semi-anarchy, permanent-death, vanilla experience on version 1.21.8.

Griefing, stealing, and PvP are allowed everywhere — anything goes within the realm of vanilla Minecraft.

The only rules exist to keep the game playable: no hacked clients, no duping, no lag machines, no exploits. Check the rules page for further details and exceptions.

Our focus has always been the community; each decision is carefully calculated in your best interest.
